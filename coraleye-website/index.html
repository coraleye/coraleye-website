About Coraleye

Why We Built Coraleye

Coraleye started with a simple observation. Customer experience is broken. Across finance, investing, and engineering, we kept watching great companies lose customers they should have kept, due to fragmented systems that prevent truly knowing the customer.

We built Coraleye because the data to prevent it already existed. It just lived in five different places and nobody was connecting it. When businesses truly know their customers, they retain more revenue, and the people on the other side of those relationships get the experience they actually deserve.

Coraleye was founded by Duane, Chris, and Arman, three college friends from Duke and Northwestern who decided to stop watching this problem happen and start solving it.

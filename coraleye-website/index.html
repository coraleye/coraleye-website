Under the surface.

The parts of Coraleye you don't see in the workbench, quietly running on every account, every day.

01

Evaluate

Score every account against evidence, not proxies.

  • Cross-references support tickets, calls, and Slack, not just login counts.
  • Re-run nightly, so a score is never more than a day stale.
  • Every score traces back to the specific interactions behind it.
02

Learn

Carry each account's history forward, automatically.

  • Renewal dates, past commitments, and prior flags stay attached to every account.
  • Nothing gets re-explained from scratch when a new signal comes in.
  • The longer Coraleye runs, the more context it has to work with.
03

Act

Draft the play. Your team makes the call.

  • Every flagged account gets a recommended play, not just a red flag.
  • Plays route to the right CSM automatically.
  • Nothing goes out without a human review step.
04

Trace

Show your work, every time.

  • Every score and play links back to the exact conversations behind it.
  • No black-box recommendations. See the evidence, not just the verdict.
  • Built for teams that need to explain a decision, not just receive one.

About Coraleye

Why we built Coraleye

Coraleye started with a simple observation: companies already have the customer data they need to prevent churn, identify expansion opportunities, and improve customer relationships. The problem is that the data lives across disconnected systems, with no unified way to understand what is actually happening across an account.

Across investing, enterprise software, and AI implementation, we repeatedly saw teams struggle to proactively manage customer relationships because conversations, support issues, product activity, and operational signals were fragmented across dozens of tools.

We built Coraleye to unify those customer signals, evaluate what matters, and help teams take action before revenue and relationships are lost.

Coraleye was founded by Duane, Chris, and Arman, three college friends from Duke and Northwestern who decided to stop watching this problem happen and start solving it.

How it works

From signal to action.

Coraleye unifies customer interactions, evaluates risk and opportunity, and surfaces the next best action for your team.

Unify All Customer Interaction Data

Coraleye connects via APIs and native integrations to gather conversations, messages, emails, calls, and third-party data so you can focus on growing relationships.

2

Cory Evaluates

Cory reads every signal across your account book and flags churn risk, expansion opportunities, sentiment shifts, and escalation patterns for your team.
